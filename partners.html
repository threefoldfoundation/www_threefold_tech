ThreeFold Foundation

Summary

ThreeFold Foundation is focused on democratizing internet compute and storage capacity to enable a responsible internet for all. This is the primary reference of ThreeFold Tech. TFTech software empowers people to create neutral, green and private capacity across the globe that’s blockchain-driven and 100% decentralized. This “hyper-distributed” network enables developers from all over the world to bring to life decentralized applications and solutions, and digital service/application providers to host their applications closer to end users.

Status

Already 18.000 CPU cores and 40 million GB of existing capacity. The grid is live for beta testing and will launch commercially in Q4 2019.

NBH-Digital

Summary

A partnership with TF Tech to build a decentralized banking system where by each user will have access to multiple digital and market assets at the palm of their hand.

Built on top of the TF Network and using 3Bots to secure both identity and digital assets. Gateway to both fiat & cryptocurrencies through the creation of a token that is fully backed by gold, and exchangeable over the counter with other cryptocurrencies (through atomic swaps).

Status

A pilot version of the decentralized wallet will launch in Q3.
Integration with Forex Markets is expected in Q4

Enertia

Summary

Project Enertia (ATWG) is focused on developing an open source decentralized protocol for electric vehicle (EV) Charging, transactions, and data sharing – all built on top of the ThreeFold platform. The ThreeFold token is used as medium of payment on the EV Grid.

Status

Engaging a UAE local authorities as a strategic partner.
Currently mapping 600+ charging stations.
Proof of concept under way in D1 tower as a customer for smart charging systems built on our grid.

In discussions with OEMs like Tesla, BMW and VW.

Flowgen

Summary

The unique design of the Flowgen turbines allows for them to achieve high output even at low windspeeds. This allows the turbines to be placed anywhere, decentralizing power supply. 

Enabling sustainable IT powered by renewable energy means overcoming one of the biggest inhibiting factors in emerging markets and allowing us to bridge the digital divide.

Status

Planning for the deployment of a joint showcase to demonstrate the viability and integration of our technologies.
Pursuing joint sales opportunities for the deployment of our decentralized renewable power + decentralized IT proposition
