Great Results on Grid 2.4 Testing!

We still need more people willing to test – join in!

10 February 2021  · 

Our TF Grid 2.4 has now been on testnet for over a month and we are getting good test results – but we need more!

We need more people who want to test, and we need more farmers who are willing to upgrade to the 2.4 (soon 2.5) release and make additional capacity available towards the community.

Anyone who is interested to contribute in writing tests or executing on tests is more than welcome – the more exposure we get, the faster our TF Grid will be usable for a more general audience!
