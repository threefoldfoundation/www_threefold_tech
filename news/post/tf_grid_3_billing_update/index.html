ThreeFold Grid 3 Billing Update

Explaining the billing process for ThreeFold Grid 3 deployments.

17 March 2022  · 

Since we are now moving more and more to Grid V3, we wanted to explain how the billing process works for deployments. Some highlights:


Grid V3 has implemented a post billing system, meaning you will get billed for the capacity you are using after you have deployed a workload. No need to spend unnecessarily for capacity you won't need.


We also provide discounts for workloads if your account has a certain amount of balance. This way you can enjoy cheaper deployments whilst you are holding TFT in your account.
