WE ARE HIRING!
Lochristi - Belgium

We are looking for an experienced Engineering Lead

Our digital world is far too CENTRALISED. Centralized hyperscale data centers are the backbone of the internet today yet 80% of the internet capacity is owned by less than 20 companies. These hyperscale data centers are unsecure, unscalable and are very power hungry. The approach of adding more people, technology layers and hence complexity is flawed. For the sake of both humanity and our planet, things need to change.

If we are to meet the demand for decentralization in the data economy, 2 key things must change.

1. Decentralisation of the compute and storage infrastructure that serves as the substrate to the economy itself, and

2. Our rights to ownership and privacy of our information (data) must be enforced. These are non negotiable

Threefold.tech takes another approach. We promise a true peer-to-peer internet, empowering equality, autonomy and sustainability with game-changing technology built with a collaborative ecosystem. ThreeFold Grid is live and already distributed across 21 countries and expanding daily.

Main Responsibilities

- Lead and align the engineering teams to promote a high-performance, results-driven environment with a strict adherence to project management requirements in a global context (Belgium, Cairo, Dubai)

- Collaborate with product management to create the product backlog

- Collaborate with the CTO and researchers to envision future possibilities

- Support Marketing in developing a strong product pipeline and a portfolio of marketable products that are differentiated and provide competitive advantage for the company.

Profile

- Experience in and good knowledge of agile methodologies in distributed teams

- Experience working with business partners and clients.

- Flexibility in working hours

- Willing to travel

- Strong technical background

- Experience working with Linux system environments

- Ability to think out of the box and to thrive in a rapidly growing startup context

What can you expect ?
